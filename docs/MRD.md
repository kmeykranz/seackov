# MRD - Underwater Extraction Treasure Run

## Audience
Players who enjoy short-session action roguelite loops and stealth-risk decisions.

## Competitive References
- Brotato-like arena readability: top-down movement, fast recognition, dense moment-to-moment decisions.
- Extraction games: carried value is unsafe until extracted.

## Marketable Hook
A flooded Earth recovery dive where every extra pickup visibly fills the backpack and increases pressure because monster sight can wipe the current-run haul, while persistent story objectives push the player from purifier repair to signal deployment, tunnel repair, ruins investigation, and a final truth escape.

## Prototype Positioning
This build validates the single-run loop, a minimal boat-side item handling loop, escalating leftward map risk/reward density, a persistent visual campaign spine on the current map, and a placeholder tool system driven by discovered story knowledge before adding final campaign art, procedural generation, or later story areas.
